# Feature List

## 1. Core Features

### A. Monthly Planner
- **Goal Input**: Users can enter goals, deadlines, habits, and tasks.
- **AI Conversion**: Automatically converts high-level goals into weekly goals, daily tasks, and calendar events.

### B. Smart Tracking
- **Status Tracking**: Mark tasks as Completed (✔️) or Skipped (❌).
- **Streaks**: Track consistency for habits and recurring tasks.
- **AI Check-ins**: Automated prompts to verify task completion (e.g., "Did you finish task A?").

### C. Calendar Integration
- **Device Integration**: Sync with Android & iOS device calendars.
- **Event Management**: Create and update calendar events directly from the app.
- **Reminders**: Local or calendar notifications to keep users on track.

## 2. AI Features

### A. Intelligence & Automation
- **Interpretation**: Understands natural language input for goals and tasks.
- **Planning Engine**: Breaks down goals into daily and weekly actionable items.
- **"Generate my month"**: Builds a complete monthly plan from a set of user goals.

### B. Adaptation & Assistance
- **Daily Briefing**: Provides a summary of today's tasks, events, and priorities.
- **Auto-reschedule**: Detects uncompleted tasks and intelligently moves them to future slots.
- **Smart Alerts**: Context-aware notifications for habits and deadlines.

## 3. User Interface (UI) & Experience (UX)

### A. Views & Dashboards
- **Calendar UI**: Toggle between Month view and Task view.
- **Progress Dashboard**: Visual overview of goal progress and completion rates.
- **Weekly Overview**: Summary of the week with AI-generated notes.

### B. Visualizations & Interactions
- **Calendar Heatmap**: Visual representation of daily progress scores.
- **Goal Cards**: Status indicators for goals (On-track / Behind).
- **Quick-add**: Fast task entry with smart AI categorization.
- **Deadline Alerts**: Visual warnings for approaching deadlines.
